GameForce

GameForce is an open-source project that aims to bring Xbox/Playstation-like achievements into Salesforce. Gamification is a proven and unobtrusive way to boost engagement and productivity by leveraging the natural competitive instinct in people. It can help employees develop their skills and be more motivated while making their job more engaging and fun.

Simple configuration

GameForce relies on a pre-built list of achievements and simple and yet robust configuration process to make sure your users can use the app and get the benefits as soon as possible

Compete with your teammates

GameForce Leaderboard facilitates competitive spirit and motivates your users to achieve more

Extend the functionality to your own liking

Create your own custom achievements and integrate GameForce with your existing business processes using Flows or Apex
